About Me

We make your business gain more revenue at a glance.

Hi, my name is Nat from Brisbane in Queensland, Australia, and I am Silver J's.

My journey has led me over the past 30 years, from creating different types of crafts to making silver jewellery.

Silver J's is a family-owned and operated jewellery business, hand-crafting from my workshop in Brisbane, Australia.

I bring to life inspiration procured from life experiences that represent my personality, which has formed a complete collection of artistically hand-crafted pieces. Silver J's has no limits to design & often includes gemstones.

I find passion in collaborating with clients to achieve personalized service, resulting in custom-designed and created pieces.

All materials I use to make my pieces are ethically sourced from reputable and transparent lapidary practices internationally with direct fair trade agreements. I use recycled Sterling Silver - stamped with my signature creative flair.

Working with precious metals, gemstones, wood, leather other natural materials runs in my blood - my Dad created beautiful horse bits and spurs of high quality that were known worldwide, a tradition kept alive by my brother today.

My hands have been busy creating many different types of craft for the past 30 years, and I have always wanted to make silver jewellery but thought it was out of my depth to learn. Events had steered me in the direction of Silversmithing. I threw myself into mastering a few of the techniques with meticulous attention to detail and found I created a beautiful wearable work of art.

I have inherited from my father the gift of bringing a vision to life using silver, gems and other materials.

My belief in hand-crafting my special pieces of jewellery is all about how it makes me feel with all the emotions of wearing something special that my heart is drawn to. When you choose one of my handmade pieces, it should bring you beauty, moments of joy and an embrace of the power of expression.

I am passionate about making my jewellery lovers experience delight and want to be their best selves. It is a connection to your soul.

Life has me believe that it's where I'm supposed to be, making jewellery.

I am so grateful to be myself and express myself through my jewellery and bringing joy to my customer.
